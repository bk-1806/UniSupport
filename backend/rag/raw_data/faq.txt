Q: What is minimum attendance required?
A: Students must maintain at least 75% attendance.

Q: Can I attend exam with low attendance?
A: Students below 75% attendance are not eligible unless approved.

Q: How to pay fees?
A: Fees can be paid through the university portal.

Q: What if I miss an exam?
A: You should contact administration for possible re-exam.

Q: How to reset student portal password?
A: Use the "Forgot Password" option on login page.
